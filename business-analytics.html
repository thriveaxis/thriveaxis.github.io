Frequently Asked Questions

What's the main difference between packages? +
Essentials: Basic dashboards and KPIs for clarity.
Growth Intelligence: Adds detailed marketing & customer analytics for growth.
Performance Optimization: Adds operational analytics and process improvement (no AI).
How long does it take to set up business analytics? +
Essentials: 2-3 weeks. Growth Intelligence: 4-5 weeks. Performance Optimization: 6-8 weeks.
Does the Performance package include AI? +
No, the Performance Optimization Suite focuses on standard BI tools and proven analytics methodologies without AI automation. This keeps costs reasonable while delivering high-value insights.
Can we upgrade our package later? +
Absolutely! You can upgrade at any time. We'll seamlessly transition your data and dashboards to the new package level.
Do you provide training for our team? +
Yes, all packages include training. Essentials has video tutorials, Growth has group sessions, and Performance includes personalized one-on-one training.

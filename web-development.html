Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website? +
The timeline depends on the package and complexity. Basic websites typically take 2-3 weeks, Advanced packages 4-5 weeks, and Pro packages 6-8 weeks or more for complex applications.
Do you provide website maintenance? +
Yes, we offer maintenance plans starting at $99/month. Our Pro package includes 3 months of maintenance. We handle updates, security, backups, and minor content changes.
Can I update the website myself? +
For Advanced and Pro packages, we integrate a Content Management System (CMS) that allows you to easily update content. We provide training on how to use it effectively.
What if I need changes after the website is completed? +
Each package includes a specific number of revision rounds. After that, we charge an hourly rate for additional changes, or you can opt for our maintenance plan.
Do you provide hosting and domain services? +
We can help you purchase domains and set up hosting. We partner with reliable hosting providers and can manage this process for you, or you can use your preferred provider.
